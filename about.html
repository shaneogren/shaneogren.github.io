Wonderstruck with what is

I tend an art nursery. Seeds appear as visions, words, melodies, forms, feelings, nudges. I plant the seeds in attention and water them with curiosity and play. Some seeds sprout.

Shane Ogren lives in Minnesota. He paints, writes songs, does woodworking, walks, gardens, and keeps up friendships. Born 1979. BFA, Kansas City Art Institute, 2002.
